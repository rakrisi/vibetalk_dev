Frequently Asked Questions

Is VibeTalk really free?

Yes! VibeTalk is completely free to use. You can chat with strangers, make connections, and enjoy all core features without paying anything. We offer optional premium features for users who want enhanced experiences.

How do I stay safe while talking to strangers online?

VibeTalk implements multiple safety features including anonymous profiles, content moderation, user blocking, and secure messaging. Never share personal information like your real name, address, or financial details unless you're absolutely certain about the person.

Can I choose who I talk to?

Absolutely. You can filter by interests, age ranges, and conversation preferences. Our matching algorithm suggests compatible partners, but you're always in control of who you connect with.

What should I talk about with strangers?

The beauty of VibeTalk is that conversations can go anywhere. Discuss hobbies, travel experiences, books, movies, life advice, or just have fun casual banter. Let the conversation flow naturally based on shared interests.

How do I report inappropriate behavior?

If you encounter someone behaving inappropriately, use the report button in the chat interface. Provide details about the concerning behavior, and our moderation team will investigate immediately.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account anytime from your account settings. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
